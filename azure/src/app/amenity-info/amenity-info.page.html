2. The swimming pool is reserved for use of Azure residents and their endorsed guests through the PMO.
3. All pool users are required to register at the registration desk. Each swimmer will be issued a disposable wristband. Guest swimmers (regardless of age) exceeding the maximum number of FREE swim band per unit (1BR = 2 FREE; 2BR = 4 FREE; 3BR = 6 FREE) must pay Php 250.00 per person per shift per day. Strictly, NO REFUND.
4. Pool users are advised to take all necessary safety precautions. The Developer / Condominium Corp. / Property Administration Office shall not be liable for any untoward incident, accident, injury or death that may arise from the use of the swimming pool.
5. Children under age of twelve (12) must be accompanied by adults at all times. Parents/Guardians must accompany and supervise their children while in the pool / pool area and remain with them for the duration thereof. Children are not allowed to swim wearing disposable or cloth diapers.
6. Proper swimwear is required. All persons should wear clothing designed as swim wear so as to prevent damage to the swimming pool filter system. 7. All types of foods and drinks shall be strictly prohibited in the pool and pool deck area.
8. Water sports are not allowed.
9. Expectorating or blowing of nose in the pool is strictly prohibited.
10. Persons observed by the pool attendants / lifeguards to have a contagious viral or skin disease, open wounds, cold sores, inflamed eyes, or wearing bandages will not be allowed in the pool.
11. The person signing the swim waiver from must be an adult, and if children are suing the swimming pool area, the form must be signed by their parent or legal guardian. The resident/s and guest/s agree to abide by the rules stated herein and in the House Rules of Azure Urban Resort Residences.
12. In any case of lightning occurrence, everyone will be required to vacate the pool areas for your safety; Purchased swim bands would still ne non-refundable.
